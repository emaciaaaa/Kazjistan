⚠️ Important Notice

Deadline Compliance: Leadership changes and expulsion notifications must be submitted within 96 hours as per Section IV & IX of the Political Party Act. Late submissions may result in penalties.

Official ECK Forms

Download, complete, and submit these forms to the Electoral Commission

Form ECK-01: Political Party Registration

Form ID: ECK-01 Reference: Section III

Initial registration form for new political parties. Requires declarations from four citizens in the public assembly channel. Submit this form within 7 days of formation.

📝 Download DOCX 📄 Download PDF

Form ECK-02: Leadership Change Notification

Form ID: ECK-02 96-HOUR DEADLINE

Mandatory notification of changes to party leadership (Leader, Deputy Leader, or Collective Leadership). Must be submitted within 96 hours as per Section IV of the Political Party Act.

📝 Download DOCX 📄 Download PDF

Form ECK-03: Party Name Change Request

Form ID: ECK-03 Reference: Section X

Formal request to change a registered party's name. New names must comply with naming rules (no government affiliation terms, 45 character limit, no inappropriate connotations).

📝 Download DOCX 📄 Download PDF

Form ECK-04: Member Expulsion Notification

Form ID: ECK-04 96-HOUR DEADLINE

Required notification when a party expels a member under its internal rules. Must be submitted within 96 hours as per Section IX. Include evidence of proper procedure.

📝 Download DOCX 📄 Download PDF

Form ECK-05: Party Dissolution Certification

Form ID: ECK-05 Reference: Section VI

Certification of party dissolution vote and seat reassignment. Requires evidence of majority vote by party members and details on elected member succession per Section VI.

📝 Download DOCX 📄 Download PDF

Form ECK-06: Party Merger Proposal

Form ID: ECK-06 Reference: Section VII

Formal merger approval request requiring majority votes in each merging party. Includes certification of votes, proposed new party details, and member list consolidation.

📝 Download DOCX 📄 Download PDF

Form ECK-07: Election Result Contestation

Form ID: ECK-07 7-DAY DEADLINE

Formal challenge to election results. Must be submitted within 7 days of result certification. Requires detailed grounds for contestation and supporting evidence.

📝 Download DOCX 📄 Download PDF

Form ECK-08: Deregistration Appeal

Form ID: ECK-08 Reference: Section VIII

Appeal process for parties threatened with deregistration due to low membership (<3) or inactivity (<100 messages/7 days). Submit evidence to maintain registration.

📝 Download DOCX 📄 Download PDF
